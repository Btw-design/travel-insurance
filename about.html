👤 About Us

About Travel Insurance Info

Our mission is to provide clear, accurate, and unbiased information about travel insurance for Indian travellers, helping you make informed decisions about protecting yourself during your journeys abroad.

Our Mission

Travel Insurance Info is a free, independent informational resource created to help Indian travellers understand travel insurance. We believe that every traveller deserves access to clear, accurate, and unbiased information about insurance products that can protect them during their journeys abroad.

We are not an insurance company, agent, broker, or seller. We do not sell policies, provide quotes, or recommend specific products or providers. Our content is researched from official IRDAI guidelines, publicly available policy documents, and government resources. We strive to present information in plain language that is accessible to all travellers, regardless of their familiarity with insurance terminology.

Our website covers the fundamental aspects of travel insurance including types of coverage, IRDAI regulations, the claim process, policy exclusions, and policyholder rights. We update our content periodically to reflect changes in IRDAI regulations and industry practices.

What We Cover

  • Travel insurance basics — what it is, how it works, and why Indian travellers need it
  • Coverage types — individual, family floater, student, senior citizen, domestic, and annual multi-trip plans
  • IRDAI regulations — policyholder rights, claim settlement timelines, grievance redressal mechanisms
  • Claim processes — step-by-step guidance on filing claims and required documentation
  • Medical coverage — understanding health benefits, evacuation coverage, and dental emergencies
  • Policy exclusions — common exclusions like pre-existing conditions, adventure sports, and high-risk activities
  • Senior citizen travel insurance — special considerations for travellers aged 60 and above
  • Student travel insurance — coverage options for Indian students studying abroad
  • COVID-19 coverage — understanding pandemic-related coverage in travel insurance policies
