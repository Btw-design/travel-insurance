Travel Insurance for Senior Citizens: Key Considerations

Informational Guide IRDAI Compliant 5 min read

Senior-Specific Coverage

Indian travellers aged 60 years and above have unique insurance needs due to higher health risks associated with age. Several Indian insurers now offer dedicated senior citizen travel insurance plans with enhanced coverage features tailored to older travellers.

Higher Medical Cover Limits

Senior travel plans typically offer medical cover up to USD 500,000 or more, recognising that older travellers face higher medical risks. Premiums reflect the increased risk but remain affordable compared to potential out-of-pocket medical expenses abroad, which can be financially devastating.

Pre-Existing Condition Coverage

Some senior travel plans offer limited coverage for acute onset of pre-existing conditions, defined as sudden and unexpected worsening of a stable condition. This is usually an add-on benefit. Chronic conditions requiring ongoing treatment are generally excluded.

Age Limits and Policy Duration

  • Maximum Entry Age: Many policies accept travellers up to 75 or 80 years. Some insurers offer policies for travellers up to 85 or 90 years with medical screening.
  • Maximum Trip Duration: Single trip limits are often lower for seniors, typically ranging from 30 to 60 days per trip.

Medical Evacuation Coverage

Senior plans often include enhanced medical evacuation and repatriation coverage. In case of serious illness, the insurer arranges and covers evacuation expenses to the nearest appropriate medical facility or back to India if medically necessary.

Tips for Senior Travellers

  • Disclose all medical conditions honestly — non-disclosure can void coverage.
  • Carry a summary of your medical history and current prescriptions.
  • Check if the policy covers acute onset of pre-existing conditions.
  • Compare multiple policies before choosing.
  • Keep emergency numbers handy at all times.
💡
Note: Senior travel insurance policies vary significantly between insurers. Always read the policy wording and schedule of benefits before purchasing.
